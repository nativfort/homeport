About

We pioneer the secure AI layer the enterprise has been waiting for.

NativFort builds the sovereign AI infrastructure that regulated organisations can actually deploy — and the higher-level products that run on top of it. Our thesis is simple: AI's biggest leverage sits on top of an organisation's most sensitive data, and the only way to unlock it is to keep that data — and the models acting on it — fully inside a trusted boundary.

We started with the hard part: a private, region-controlled, audit-ready core. Every product we ship — beginning with Secure Document Intelligence — inherits its guarantees. That's how we make customers AI-ready and AI-powered without asking them to relax a single security control.
