The problem

Enterprises must become AI-powered — without surrendering their data.

The most valuable AI use cases sit on top of the most sensitive corporate data — contracts, deal rooms, privileged communications, employee records, regulated client files. Public AI assistants weren't built for that. They train on prompts, blur tenant boundaries, and offer no real audit trail.

Security, legal and compliance teams are stuck saying no. Business teams ship shadow tools anyway. The result: leaked context, regulatory exposure, and AI programs that stall before they create real leverage.

NativFort closes that gap. AI-ready and AI-powered, with privacy and security that is unmatched.
